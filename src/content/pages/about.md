Hi, I'm **Hiep Lam**. This is **memo** — my personal blog and notebook where I
write up things I'm researching and learning: software architecture, terminals
and low-level systems, and whatever else I happen to be digging into.

Posts here lean toward long-form deep dives and explainers, often in both
English and Vietnamese.

## Colophon

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build/) and
[Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/), on top of the
[AstroPaper](https://github.com/satnaing/astro-paper) theme by Sat Naing.
